Rules

    Basic Rules of QuestCraft, Edition 2.0(Simplified)

    Administration SLA:

  1. Administration is required to uphold the following rules, and are responsible for all griefings etc… against QuestCraft players.
  2. All QuestCraft Staff/Administration are required to assist Players in game and in discord
  3. All Staff are required to solve and set consequences for the breaking of rules
  4. all punishable acts must have some form of evidence against the incriminated player

    5. Chat Rules:

  5. It is forbidden to use any forms of Swearing, make up conflict based on personal beliefs(religion, politics etc…)
  6. it is forbidden to make false statements about QuestCraft, or act as administration
  7. All forms of advertising are forbidden,
  8. Use common sense and keep all chat messages appropriate for all users

    9. Gaming Process:

  9. It is forbidden to create structures or use client side add ons to destabilize the server in any way/
  10. All forms of griefing, hacking, or harming the builds of others are prohibited
  11. It is forbidden to create/wear any forms of content containing: erotic elements, Nazi symbols, insults, mats, prison slang.
  12. Pranks are allowed, But they must not violate any of the QuestCraft rules.

    13. Building:

  13. it is forbidden to build any illogical structure, or a structure that is disrespectful or is meant to look incorrect/bad.

    14. Discord:

  14. all in game rules apply in discord
  15. all QuestCraft players are required to have joined the QuestCraft discord server
