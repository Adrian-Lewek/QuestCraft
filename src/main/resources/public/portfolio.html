Bias In Tech Research Paper

In my bias in tech paper i researched biases in googles and other search engines algorimths. A bias in technology creates unfair divides between people, based on a rule that technology follows. Often created intentionally by humans. in my research i found that bias in search algorithms, can shield people from info, make assumptions or only show people things based on a artificial group they are put in. One of the biggest biases in search algorithms, is googles promoted answer. lots of times these textboxes you see are just a algorithm deciding if thats what you want to hear, not necesarly a correct answer.
