About

QuestCraft is a small survival minecraft server that was first released on Tuesday, July 9th, 2019. originally created by chestly with insparation from Simple Survival and HermitCraft. As it grows it continues to gain more popularity not only amoung the administrators friends, but also on planet minecraft.

As a early server QuestCraft was mainly based of the idea of a place where players could come, and not face internet drama, and have a respectful place to build make friends and have fun. Simple Survival is a similar minecraft server, but with opposite views of respect and how to treat players.

Now QuestCraft has become much more popular and with the increase of players comes more advertisement online, and more fees to be payed. the vision of questcraft contines though, and it will continue to be a place of freedom and respect for all players.
